WHO WE ARE
Continental Trading Enterprise is one of the leading business conglomerate which was established more than two decades ago in Nepal. We believe in delivering the best class brands and unbridled services to our customers and clients over Nepal. We are associated with the four popular brands like Komatsu construction and mining equipment, KIA passenger vehicles, Otis elevators and drilling equipment’s and Isuzu commercial vehicles.
OUR VISION
Focused towards best quality services prior to customer satisfaction, community betterment, progress and innovation.
OUR MISSION
Committed to excel in distributing satisfying and prominent solutions, best technologies, innovation and quality service according to the needs of customers.
OUR CORE VALUES
Our core values for thriving in growth of nation and focusing onto the corporate social responsibility inspires our staffs in the way of working.

OUR STORY
It was not before than twenty years ago a group of highly energetic entrepreneurs started the foundation with a clear conviction to excel in the field of trading enterprise. Continental began with construction, bulk transportation, organic farming and social service. Not long after the establishment, we became the authorized distributors for Kia in Nepal. Soon, we were associated with the most popular brands in Nepal in the sector of automobile, construction, industrial equipment and financial services.
